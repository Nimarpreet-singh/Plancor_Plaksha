Sustainability at the Core

At Plancor, we're transforming agricultural waste into beautiful home decor, creating a sustainable cycle that helps both our planet and rural communities.

Discover Our Impact

Our Sustainable Process

From farm waste to beautiful home decor, our process is designed to be environmentally friendly at every step.

Collection

We collect agricultural waste that would otherwise be burned or discarded.

Processing

The waste is processed using eco-friendly methods to create our base materials.

Crafting

Skilled artisans transform the materials into beautiful decor pieces.

Eco-Packaging

Products are packaged using recyclable and biodegradable materials.
